Internet Usage 101 by Landon

When navigating through the world wide web, there are certain precautions that we must take in order to keep ourselves safe. The internet world is full of not just hackers but so many other traps that are dying to steal your information. It is not just as simple as downloading something suspicious. Its not even as simple as typing in a bit too much information on a website, it can get extremely complex.
Firstly let us discuss what to do if you are in a foreign place, say on vacation, or just anywhere outside home. You should always try to use a VPN. It helps so much to cover you. Not connecting to public wifi is a wise way to go as well. You should also always have your passwords written down so that you do not have to spend a lot of time possibly resetting them while you're there.
However, just some overall tips for everyday life are good and timeless. Be careful to click on cookies and clicking on anything that might redirect you somewhere. Be careful when downloading anything and make sure it's not from any malware site. Most importantly, use strong unique passwords with uppercase letters and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters !@#. Have fun browsing!
