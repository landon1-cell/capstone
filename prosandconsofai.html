    • Dr. Walker's Final stance on AI

    "As a professor, I have come across the discussions on AI more than i have fingers and toes. There are so many ways to get your brain flowing with creative ideas it just isn't worth it to me. Not only that but it is harming the enviornment everytime someone just types in a simple prompt. One day we might have a less cleanly earth because of it." - Dr. Walker, 2026
